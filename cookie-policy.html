1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and store information about your preferences and browsing behavior.

Cookies are essential to the functioning of modern websites and help us provide you with a better user experience. We use cookies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve our services.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. Without these cookies, services you've asked for cannot be provided.

Cookie Type: Session & Security Cookies
Duration: Session (cleared when browser closes)
Purpose: Authentication, fraud prevention, site security

Performance & Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. These cookies collect information about how you interact with our website, including which pages you visit, how long you stay, and what links you click.

Cookie Type: Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat)
Duration: 2 years
Purpose: Track visitor behavior, page performance, user demographics

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember choices you make to provide a more personalized experience. This includes language preferences, display settings, and other user customizations.

Cookie Type: Preference & Personalization Cookies
Duration: 6 months to 2 years
Purpose: Remember user preferences, language, theme selections

Marketing & Targeting Cookies

These cookies track your browsing patterns to deliver relevant content and advertisements. They may be set by third-party advertising partners.

Cookie Type: Third-party Marketing Cookies
Duration: Varies by provider (typically 6 months - 2 years)
Purpose: Deliver targeted ads, track conversion, build user profiles

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:
  • Ensure the website functions correctly and securely
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze traffic and understand user behavior through Google Analytics
  • Improve the design, content, and functionality of our website
  • Deliver personalized content and recommendations
  • Track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

Google Analytics

MIHP uses Google Analytics to collect website usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies to store information such as the time your visit occurred, whether you have been to the site before, and what site referred you to our webpage.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Bootstrap & CDN Services

We load CSS and JavaScript libraries from Content Delivery Networks (CDNs). These services may set cookies for performance tracking and delivery optimization.

5. Cookie Control & Your Rights

You have the right to control cookies on your device. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. To learn how to manage cookies in your browser, visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
Note: Disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our website. Some features may not work properly without cookies.

6. Data Retention

We retain cookie data as follows:

  • Session Cookies: Automatically deleted when you close your browser
  • Essential Cookies: Retained for security and functionality purposes (typically 1-2 years)
  • Analytics Cookies: Retained for up to 2 years (Google Analytics default)
  • Preference Cookies: Retained for 6 months to 2 years
  • Marketing Cookies: Retained per third-party provider policies

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at contact@mihp.ph.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of the website following any updates constitutes your acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
